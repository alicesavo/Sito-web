Hi! I'm Alice!



My name is Alice, I'm 20 years old and I graduated in photography at the higher education institution Giovanni Falcone in Gallarate.

My passion for photography was born from my grandmother who, from an early age, worked in photographic studios and photographed everything around her. He still shoots with his camera.

Thanks to the school I was able to participate in various photographic projects and not, such as: the course with Rai Porte Aperte, internships at the museum MAGA (Gallarate), competition "Words are light", writing chapter "Relay creative writing", internship at "Zanella Production"...

I learned to relate to other people and work with them, understanding more and more how I could best approach the world of work.

During the pandemic (last two school years), we did much more graphic work than photography and I became more and more curious. They even made us build a website with WordPress!
From this moment I understood what I really wanted to do as a job.

After the school I decided to take and follow the UX/UI Design course of Start2Impact.
